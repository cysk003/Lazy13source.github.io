Res-download的下载原理是代理电脑网络，从网络里嗅探视频资源，进而实现下载

所以安装软件后需要安装代理证书

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/HautRxjCevvC3j3iaFnMew5cPXQtElfNR1qsXyHAQibFV1ToJkrWfzUNzmFLhffT5POdt8Wfib7wnk1cDRgfcViaxg/640?wx_fmt=other&from;=appmsg&tp;=webp&wxfrom;=5&wx;_lazy=1&wx;_co=1)

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/HautRxjCevvC3j3iaFnMew5cPXQtElfNRNO1vus8ichCjDFEUsJw2B1Haz5l6zfJMqEmbR74SkeAqsJ6GGtnFJxA/640?wx_fmt=other&from;=appmsg&tp;=webp&wxfrom;=5&wx;_lazy=1&wx;_co=1)

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/HautRxjCevvC3j3iaFnMew5cPXQtElfNRLeSm5UmJh96JVSOM2ib5bXk1ynTYfYJrtviblSaDJKNicC6vGl0Sg04GA/640?wx_fmt=other&from;=appmsg&tp;=webp&wxfrom;=5&wx;_lazy=1&wx;_co=1)
