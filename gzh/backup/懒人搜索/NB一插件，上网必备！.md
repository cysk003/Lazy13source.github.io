## codebox

平台：浏览器插件

本插可以搞定多个平台的复制文档，以及解除强制登录才能访问内容

具体功能有

对于CSDN

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/BXJXNRRKQNIiaIzGibibXLibokTXT9BdnribxwEsnoeVxVlSdDQ7M54KgPNMVB81yiatf81dchxeRKCtGiae1iaK8YCBcw/640?wx_fmt=png&from;=appmsg)

对于知乎百度和简书可以

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/BXJXNRRKQNIiaIzGibibXLibokTXT9BdnribxnvtFWe0TbcbtNQcVhPALj2YCjrIjibicWU9GWXhib2qXicOlW9nNKiay23Q/640?wx_fmt=png&from;=appmsg)

脚本之家和博客园

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/BXJXNRRKQNIiaIzGibibXLibokTXT9BdnribxP8ehvwXpb8dHMZwsdVTmHanuykQNVIBatkHEO6nsR4eyl6yBcOXynw/640?wx_fmt=png&from;=appmsg)

51CTO和Php中文网

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/BXJXNRRKQNIiaIzGibibXLibokTXT9BdnribxlPYiamok3yrOys9hxt8K2hAWW7wDlY3tjz6GDn2LNABK2U0GGVzNOLQ/640?wx_fmt=png&from;=appmsg)

帮助大家更畅快得在中文互联网里畅游和复制代码

安装插件后还可以自定义哪些网站开启

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/BXJXNRRKQNIiaIzGibibXLibokTXT9BdnribxOUnYtAka7LT1kxbOzAYwRIAJo4EfU4zqph9WpYAldZicnc4nYmLOaSQ/640?wx_fmt=png&from;=appmsg)

功能强大，还是有必要备一个的~
