## 2.替换引擎版

如果你的X雷遇到这种需要升级到新版本后才能下载

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/VSpOBPbVdV5wUUibcAKyx7nzZoibOvrB3gqXVuhBkk2Vs5danVrCSZsvsK2NScF3Px5XnTHr2TlafmJ9E9drrWmA/640?wx_fmt=other&from;=appmsg&tp;=webp&wxfrom;=5&wx;_lazy=1&wx;_co=1)

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/VSpOBPbVdV5wUUibcAKyx7nzZoibOvrB3gGOGqkaG8LQq6AXoGho6pqbqj42UeIdSNcLac4cXuunRcIDzgqF2m5A/640?wx_fmt=other&from;=appmsg&tp;=webp&wxfrom;=5&wx;_lazy=1&wx;_co=1)图片

`Thunder\Program\resources\bin\SDK`里的文件

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/VSpOBPbVdV5wUUibcAKyx7nzZoibOvrB3gLiccClKqJjj1JT3z3uq2MkqTtYlsDvDqXOGrXnybmTREpEB3eSibs5Nw/640?wx_fmt=other&from;=appmsg&tp;=webp&wxfrom;=5&wx;_lazy=1&wx;_co=1)图片
